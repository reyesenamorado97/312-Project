How to Play

Battleship is a game where two players compete to try to sink their opponent's ship first! This game is a simplified version where the location of your ship and your opponent's ship has already been chosen for you. It is represented by one coordinate in the four by four grid that appears on your screen. Go ahead and select any button labeled 1 to 16 to guess the location of your opponent's ship. Once you have fired a canon towards it, it can either splash in the water or be a successful hit. In the case of the latter, a victory message will appear, letting you know that you have won the game and your progress will be saved on the leader board. If you miss however, you must wait for your opponent to make their guess on the location of your ship before you are ready to fire again.
